1. Watermelon Wave Spritz (Vodka)

A truly refreshing drink for a hot day. This isn't a pre-made sugary mess; it's light, bubbly, and vibrant with real fruit flavor.

Recommended Spirit: Reyka Vodka

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Reyka Vodka
  • 3 oz fresh watermelon juice (muddle cubes of watermelon and strain)
  • 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  • 2-3 fresh mint leaves
  • Chilled prosecco or sparkling wine, to top

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, gently muddle the mint leaves.
  2. Add the vodka, fresh watermelon juice, and fresh lime juice.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake for about 15 seconds, or until the shaker is well-chilled.
  4. Strain the mixture into a tall glass filled with fresh ice.
  5. Top the drink with chilled prosecco or sparkling wine. Garnish with a small watermelon wedge or a fresh mint sprig.

2. The Sunset Sipper (Tequila)

This cocktail takes the classic Paloma to the next level. The subtle heat and tartness create a complex and beautiful flavor that's perfect for sipping as the sun goes down.

Recommended Spirit: Casamigos Blanco Tequila

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Casamigos Blanco Tequila
  • 3 oz fresh grapefruit juice
  • 0.5 oz fresh lime juice
  • 0.25 oz agave nectar (or simple syrup)
  • Pinch of chili powder and a lime wheel, for garnish
  • Grapefruit soda, to top

Instructions:

  1. Rub the rim of a tall glass with a lime wedge and dip it in chili powder.
  2. Fill the glass with ice.
  3. Add the tequila, fresh grapefruit juice, fresh lime juice, and agave nectar.
  4. Stir the ingredients to combine, and then top with grapefruit soda.
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel.

3. Boardwalk Old Fashioned (Bourbon)

For when the sun is gone and the conversation deepens. This is still a true Old Fashioned, but the subtle addition of a spiced liqueur or a dash of coffee adds a whole new dimension of warmth and complexity.

Recommended Spirit: Maker's Mark Bourbon

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Maker's Mark Bourbon
  • 0.5 oz coffee liqueur (like Kahlúa) OR 2 dashes of spiced cherry bitters
  • 1 dash Angostura bitters
  • Orange peel, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. Add the bourbon, coffee liqueur (or spiced bitters), and Angostura bitters to a mixing glass filled with ice.
  2. Stir for about 30 seconds, or until the mixture is well-chilled. The drink should be silky and smooth.
  3. Strain the mixture into a rocks glass with one large ice cube.
  4. Express the oils from the orange peel over the drink by twisting it and then drop it in.

4. The Bourbon Beach Smash (Bourbon)

A proper smash is all about muddling fresh ingredients to release their flavors. It's an interactive drink that's both refreshing for the heat and complex enough to enjoy as an evening sipper.

Recommended Spirit: Bulleit Bourbon

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Bulleit Bourbon
  • 2 lemon wedges
  • 4-6 fresh mint leaves
  • 0.75 oz simple syrup

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, combine the lemon wedges and mint leaves. Gently muddle them together to release the juices and oils.
  2. Add the bourbon and simple syrup.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake vigorously for about 15 seconds.
  4. Strain the mixture into a rocks glass filled with fresh crushed ice.
  5. Garnish with a fresh mint sprig and a lemon wheel.

5. Spicy Mango Tango (Tequila)

A bold and zesty cocktail that brings a little heat to the party. The fresh mango sweetness is perfectly balanced by the tequila and a touch of jalapeño.

Recommended Spirit: Don Julio Blanco Tequila

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Don Julio Blanco Tequila
  • 2 oz fresh mango puree (or muddled fresh mango)
  • 0.75 oz fresh lime juice
  • 1 thin slice of fresh jalapeño
  • Lime wheel or mango slice, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, gently muddle the jalapeño slice.
  2. Add the tequila, mango puree, and fresh lime juice.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake hard for 15-20 seconds.
  4. Strain into a rocks glass with fresh ice.
  5. Garnish with a lime wheel or a mango slice.

6. The Agave & Berry Breeze (Tequila)

This is the ultimate light, refreshing drink for a daytime celebration. It's a vibrant, colorful mix of fresh fruit, with the tequila providing a perfect, clean base.

Recommended Spirit: Patrón Silver Tequila

Ingredients:

  • 2 oz Patrón Silver Tequila
  • 4-5 fresh berries (raspberries, strawberries, or a mix)
  • 0.75 oz fresh lime juice
  • 0.5 oz agave nectar
  • Club soda or sparkling water, to top
  • Berry and mint sprig, for garnish

Instructions:

  1. In a shaker, gently muddle the fresh berries.
  2. Add the tequila, fresh lime juice, and agave nectar.
  3. Fill the shaker with ice and shake until well-chilled.
  4. Strain into a tall glass with fresh ice.
  5. Top with club soda or sparkling water. Garnish with a fresh berry and a mint sprig.
